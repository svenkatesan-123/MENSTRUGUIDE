ANEMIA

Anemia is a disorder when there is a reduction in the quantity of haemoglobin or red blood cells in the blood, which affects the blood's capacity to carry oxygen to the body's tissues. There are various forms of anaemia, and the underlying reason may influence the course of treatment. Nonetheless, the following are some natural treatments for anaemia management:

1. Foods high in iron

Iron is necessary for the development of red blood cells. Spinach, lentils, tofu, beans, dark chocolate, and beef are a few examples of foods high in iron.

2. Foods high in vitamin C

Vitamin C aids in the absorption of iron. Oranges, strawberries, kiwis, and broccoli are a few examples of foods high in vitamin C.

3. Beetroot

A good source of iron, folate, and vitamin C is beetroot. Manage anaemia by consuming beetroot juice or including it in salads.

4. Pomegranate

Pomegranates are high in vitamin C and iron. Pomegranate juice or pomegranate seeds may be useful for treating anaemia.

5. Blackstrap molasses

Blackstrap molasses is an excellent source of potassium, calcium, and iron. It may aid in the treatment of anaemia if it is added to smoothies, oatmeal, or other foods.

6. Nettle tea

Tea made from nettles: Tea made from nettles is a natural source of iron and vitamin C. Nettle tea may be used to treat anaemia.

7. Avoid caffeine and tea

Avoid caffeine and tea since they both contain substances that could prevent iron from being absorbed. It is advised to avoid or consume these beverages in moderation.

It is crucial to remember that home treatments might not be adequate to treat severe cases of anaemia, and that you should seek medical counsel instead.
