ABSENCE OF PERIODS

It's crucial to remember that in order to determine the underlying cause and receive the proper therapy if you are having a prolonged absence of periods, you should speak with a medical practitioner. But, the following natural therapies could assist you in controlling your menstrual cycle:

1. Exercise frequently

Exercise can enhance blood circulation and hormone regulation, which may aid your menstrual cycle.

2. Manage stress

Take steps to reduce stress because it can mess with your hormone levels and lead to irregular periods. Consider stress-relieving exercises, yoga, meditation, or deep breathing.

3. Maintain a healthy weight

Keep a healthy weight because it can interrupt your menstrual cycle to be overweight or underweight. By eating a balanced diet and exercising frequently, you can keep your weight at a healthy level.

4. Ginger

Traditionally, ginger has been used to control menstrual periods. You can take ginger supplements, drink ginger tea, or add ginger to cuisine.

5. Cinnamon

Studies have shown that cinnamon improves insulin sensitivity, which in turn regulates menstrual periods. You can have cinnamon tea or add it to your diet.

6. Fennel

Fennel seeds have been used for regulating menstrual cycles in the past. Fennel seeds can be chewed or used to make tea.

7. Papaya

Papaya is thought to increase the production of oestrogen and assist control menstrual periods. Papaya juice or ripe papaya can be consumed.

Keep in mind that home cures should not take the place of medical care. See a doctor if you are suffering a prolonged period of not having your period or if you have any other concerns.
