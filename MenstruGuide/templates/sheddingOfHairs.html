How to get rid of shedding of hairs during menstruation

Shedding of hair during menstruation is a common occurrence due to hormonal fluctuations. However, there are some steps you can take to help minimize hair shedding during your period:

1. Eat a healthy diet : A balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help support hair growth and reduce shedding. Make sure to include foods that are high in iron, such as leafy greens and red meat, to help maintain healthy levels of hemoglobin and prevent anemia.

2. Reduce stress : Stress can cause hormonal imbalances, which can lead to increased hair shedding. Practice stress-reducing activities such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises to help manage stress levels.

3. Use gentle hair care products : Harsh chemicals and styling tools can damage hair and make it more prone to shedding. Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that are free from sulfates and parabens, and avoid excessive heat styling.

4. Massage your scalp : Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth and reduce shedding.

5. Get enough rest : Getting enough rest is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep per night to help reduce stress and support overall hair health.

6. Talk to your healthcare provider : If you are experiencing severe hair shedding during your period, it may be a good idea to talk to your healthcare provider. They may recommend additional treatments or tests to help manage your symptoms.
