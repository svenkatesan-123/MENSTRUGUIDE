Yoga for healthy skin during menstruation

Yoga can be a helpful practice for promoting healthy skin during periods. Here are some yoga poses that can be beneficial:

1. Child's Pose (Balasana) : This pose helps to reduce stress and tension, which can improve overall skin health.

2. Forward Fold (Uttanasana) : This pose increases blood flow to the face and can help to reduce puffiness and dark circles.

3. Fish Pose (Matsyasana) : This pose stretches the neck and chest and can help to improve circulation and skin tone.

4. Triangle Pose (Trikonasana) : This pose helps to increase blood flow and circulation to the face and can help to reduce puffiness and dark circles.

5. Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana) : This pose helps to improve digestion and can help to reduce acne and breakouts.

6. Legs Up the Wall Pose (Viparita Karani) : This pose helps to reduce stress and tension and can help to improve overall skin health.

In addition to these yoga poses, it's important to stay hydrated, eat a healthy diet, and get plenty of rest during your period to support overall skin health.
