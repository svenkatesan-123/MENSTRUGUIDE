Frequently Asked Question

How can I keep track of data pertaining to my health and well-being (such as my periods and symptoms)?
You can add, edit, or delete your periods, symptoms, and events by clicking the '+' icon, with the exception of events that are imported automatically from third-party trackers. Also you can delete historical data at any time by reaching out to our customer support team at support@menstruguide.health. We firmly believe that our users deserve complete control over their data, and we are here to support you every step of the way.
Can I change my lifestyle settings and information about my past pregnancies?
Yes, you can edit your lifestyle settings and your past pregnancies. You can delete your past pregnancies as well.
How can I change data I used while signing up (like email address or a password)?
You can edit your email address if it's not verified and change your password.
You can change your password by signing out of your registered account and tapping the “Forgot your password” button on the login screen. You can also edit your email address
Can I withdraw my consent from processing my data?
TWhere we process your personal data with your consent, you can withdraw your consent at any time if you want us to stop.
