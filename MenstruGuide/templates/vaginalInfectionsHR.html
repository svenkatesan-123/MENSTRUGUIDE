VAGINAL INFECTION HOME REMEDIES

CAUSES:

Candida is a fungus that causes vaginal yeast infections. These yeasts are often found in modest amounts throughout the body. They cause no problem when they are in balance in our body’s ecosystem. However, when the balance gets disrupted, they grow rapidly and cause a yeast infection. This condition is also known as vulvovaginal candidiasis or vaginal candidiasis.

Some of the elements that may cause vaginal yeast infections include:

  • Tight-fitting clothing
  • Use of sanitary pads
  • Use of intrauterine devices (IUDs)
  • Spermicidal creams
  • Certain medicines like high-spectrum antibiotics and systemic corticosteroids
  • Highdoses of oral contraceptives
  • Allergies and allergic rhinitis (allergies causing itching, watery eyes, runny nose, etc.)
  • Spermicidal creams

    1. Diet Modification :

    Consuming yeast-based bread has been linked in studies to recurrent vaginal yeast infections. According to a different study, consumption of refined carbohydrates may contribute to the worsening of vaginal yeast infection symptoms. Vaginal yeast infections may be treated with a diet low in yeast and processed sugars. To back up these findings, more study is necessary.

    2. Garlic

    Perhaps garlic has antifungal properties. Garlic may be able to treat vaginal yeast infections, according to studies. Allicin, the active ingredient in garlic, may inhibit the growth of fungi. To demonstrate its efficacy, more studies on humans and animals are needed. Moreover, it could result in chemical burns or allergic responses. Hence before using, please seek medical advice.

    3. Tea tree oil

    According to several research, tea tree oil may have the ability to function as a natural antifungal and antibacterial agent. It might be beneficial to use tea tree pessaries, a medication capsule placed into the vagina, or tea tree oil in the bath. They might also be offered as creams and gels. There is a potential that it will irritate certain people extremely badly. In a study, it was hypothesised that the vagina's acidic environment would affect tea tree oil's ability to work. Because of this, each individual may experience different affects on the vagina.

    4. Probiotics

    Good bacteria (probiotics) might help against vaginal yeast infections. The recolonisation (refilling the lost bacteria) of bacteria, especially lactobacillus, might be helpful. Therefore, your doctor might recommend eating yoghurt, probiotic capsules, etc. Sometimes, your doctor might prescribe you probiotic creams that have to be applied to the vagina or inserted into it.

    7. Coriander seeds

    According to India's official health portal, coriander seeds may be effective in treating vaginal yeast infections. You must soak some coriander seeds in water overnight before using them.
